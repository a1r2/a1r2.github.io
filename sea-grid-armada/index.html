About the game

The grid is not the game. Sea Grid Armada takes the old idea of naval guessing and layers real tactical systems on top — earned visibility, committed shots, and an endgame that rewards pattern reading over luck.

Built for face-to-face play: no servers, no accounts, no internet after download. Just two opponents, one local link, and the fog between you. Developed by A1R2 for iPhone and iPad.
