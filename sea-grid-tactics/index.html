About Sea Grid Tactics™

Classic naval combat meets modern mobile gaming. We took the timeless strategy of Battleship and reimagined it for today's connected world—with thoughtful mechanics that keep every turn exciting.

Built for face-to-face gaming: airports, flights, coffee shops, family gatherings. No servers, no accounts, no internet required (after download). Just pure tactical fun between two opponents.
